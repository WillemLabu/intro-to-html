Everest

On the morning of May 10, 1996, climbers (Jason Clarke and Josh Brolin) from two expeditions start their final ascent towards the summit of Mount Everest, the highest point on Earth. With little warning, a violent storm strikes the mountain, engulfing the adventurers in one of the fiercest blizzards ever encountered by man.Challenged by the harshest conditions imaginable, the teams must endure blistering winds and freezing temperatures in an epic battle to survive nearly against impossible odds.

Reviews

Everest is chalk full of star power. Everyone is believable in this hostile environment,
going on from optimistic and adventurous to mortified and forced to fight for their lives.
The visuals are stunning, the shots they get of climbers and the way the camera gives you an idea of how dangerous this is are breath taking.
The cinematography is definitely award worthy.
